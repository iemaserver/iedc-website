### 4. Mission

> **Mission Statement**
> “The IEDC at IEM is committed to fostering a culture of research and product development among students. Its primary objective is to nurture a student community proficient in cutting-edge technologies through creativity and innovation. Under the mentorship of faculty members, students at the IEDC work in diverse fields including Robotics, IoT, RF & Microwave, Machine Learning, AI, Augmented Reality, Virtual Reality, and unmanned aerial and ground vehicles. The center strives for excellence by developing industry-ready products with significant real-world impact. Additionally, it focuses on cultivating entrepreneurship and tackling technological and business challenges faced by startups.”
